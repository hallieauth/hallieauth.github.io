About Me

Hi! My name is Hallie Auth. I am a junior at Boise State University studying communications and art history. I am also working to get my certifcate in digital media. I am an amateur photographer and videographer and I try to combine that with my passion for being outside. I grew up in Cleveland, Ohio and my family moved to McCall, Idaho when I was a junior in high school. It was in Idaho that I realized how much the outdoor community had to offer. I had grown up skiing but I found myself starting to learn how to mountain bike, hike, and rock climb. It was in these activites I learned how much I loved documenting them not only just participating in them. For my senior project in high school my dad helped me start McCall Life a magazine and website that focused on the outdoor community in McCall. On my media page you'll see examples of the work I did for the website!
